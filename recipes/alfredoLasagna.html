Lasagna

Chicken Alfredo Lasagna is a creamy, indulgent twist on the traditional lasagna. Layers of tender shredded chicken, velvety ricotta, and fresh spinach are nestled between sheets of pasta and smothered in a rich homemade Alfredo sauce made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. Melted mozzarella blankets the top, turning golden and bubbly in the oven. The result is a comforting, savory dish that’s elegant enough for guests but cozy enough for a family dinner. It’s perfect for those who love creamy pasta dishes and want something a little different than the classic tomato-based lasagna.

  • Alfredo sauce (any kind you prefer)
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken (rotisserie works well)
  • 1 box lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup baby spinach (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Mix ricotta with egg, salt, pepper, and spinach if using.
  2. Spread a little Alfredo sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles. Spread some ricotta, alfredo sauce, chicken and mozzarella. Repeat layers (usually 3 times). Finish with sauce and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  3. Bake at 375°F (190°C). Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  4. Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  5. Rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with parsley.
